Our Beginning

A single parent faces a myriad of issues. For a single parent, having access to basic necessities is especially important when babies are at their growing phase. However, the cost of raising babies has been exponentially increasing in the recent years. The cost of raising a baby aged 0-2 in Singapore can amount to a whopping $60000 due to the high costs of basic essentials. For single parents, being able to afford such a large amount of money is extremely difficult. Furthermore, the Covid-19 situation has exacerbated this issue as many working adults have lost their jobs or have been receiving salary cuts. As such, through our website, we hope to reduce the financial burden of these single parents by providing them an avenue to get free baby products easily

Our Mission

Brand New Baby is a website designed to connect both donors and single parents, helping parents acquire basic necessities for their children. Every child has the right to grow up healthy and we endeavor to provide them with the means to do so via aid to their parents

Our Vision

To be the leading charity in Singapore devoted to cater to single parents through efficient distribution of resources.

Our Values

As we work towards our mission, we embrace the values of compassion, efficiency and safety

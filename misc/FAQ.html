Frequently Asked Questions

Brand New Baby hopes to assist single parents in acquiring necessities for their baby. We are currently addressing the needs of parents with babies aged 1 year and below.

You can contribute by signing up to be a donor. As a donor, you can view the requests made by the beneficiaries for specific items and respond to these requests.

As of now, Brand New Baby will not accept item requests and donations outside of what is stated in the donation form. However as the website gains popularity, we have plans to expand the items available for donation. Therefore, please check our website regularly.
